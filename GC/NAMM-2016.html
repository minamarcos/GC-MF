For a musician, there's no more exciting time of year than NAMM! At Guitar Center, we spend months before the event looking forward to finding out what the National Association of Music Merchants has in store for us - and, more importantly, for you. That's why we'll be keeping close tabs on all the latest developments, and when the newest instruments and gear are ready for primetime, look for them to appear right here on our virtual shelves. After all, there's nothing we'd love more than to give you the opportunity to own that "gotta-have-it" new release from the moment it's unveiled at the show! And if there's anything that past years have taught us, it's that NAMM 2016 is sure to bring us some truly amazing gear.

You've got only one question to answer before you dive in to start filling up your 2016 wish list: what kind of equipment are you looking for? Of course, you don't have to be too specific, but it'll definitely help to have a ballpark idea of what interests you. That could mean the latest Fender or Gibson reissue, a feature-packed DJ controller, top-tier studio headphones, PA speakers or just about anything else for that matter! If it's used by musicians and audio professionals, then there's every chance it will make an appearance at NAMM 2016 - and when it does, we'll put it right here for you to find.

So get your collection ready for an overhaul, and clear a little space in your jam room to squeeze in that new guitar, amplifier, pedal or stand. We're ready and waiting to bring NAMM debuts onto Guitar Center shelves as soon as they hit the floor at the show, and we don't expect them to stay on those shelves for long! NAMM arrivals are usually hot items, and the more work we have to do shipping them, the better - because that means they're finding homes with musicians and pros like you, ready to use them to revolutionize the musical landscape.
