Starts Tomorrow

No matter where in the nation you live, if you're a musician, there's one event worth getting hyped about more than all others: NAMM. In case you're not familiar, that stands for the National Association of Music Merchants, and it's the trade show where instrument builders and pro audio suppliers step up to the plate with all their latest and greatest creations. At Musician's Friend, our top priority is to get in on the ground floor to bring you NAMM-related news as it breaks - and that's not all. We've put together a great list of gear inspired by the show, including early releases and some of the all-stars from 2015, that you can add to your collection right now.

Sometimes the best way to prep for the NAMM show is to take a look back at what last year's event brought to the table. There's no time like the present to pick up that amazing instrument or accessory you've been pining after all year, and there's plenty to choose from, in categories as diverse as headphones - the Blue Mo-Fi Powered High-Fidelity Headphones for instance - and acoustic guitars, such as the Martin CEO-8 Sitka Spruce Grand Auditorium Acoustic Guitar. And that's just the tip of an iceberg that's also packed with pedals, amps, speakers and more.

If you live by the mantra "never look back" and you'd rather set your sights firmly on the future, why not check out the NAMM 2016 early releases? Some of the most sought-after brands have put their newest gear on our shelves in advance of the show, so you can beat the rush and get them hot off the presses, so to speak. For example, Fender and Gibson are going head-to-head with the Jimi Hendrix Stratocaster in Olympic White and the 2016 Les Paul Standard Electric Guitar in light burst. Prefer to live your passion in the booth? Maybe the Pioneer DDJ-RZ 4-Channel Rekordbox DJ Controller will be the one for you.

Go ahead and take a look through the NAMM 2016 lineup to see all the options! With fantastic finds leading up to this year's show as well as a great selection of last year's top performers, you just might need to add a few lines to your wish list... or skip that step altogether and just take them straight to the checkout! It's all up to you, and if you'd like to make this the year to revitalize your collection of instruments and equipment, then you're in the perfect place to get started.
